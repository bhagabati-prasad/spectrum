Who are we ?

We let you push your limit to improve your skill for a better future.

SPECTRUM ,Technical society of COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY, BHUBANESWAR,a place for all technical enthusiasts to learn, discover and innovate new things in the field of Technology and Design. The name of the club SPECTRUM, a contribution of our alumni of 2015 batch, is particularly used to describe the characteristic colors of visible light after passing through a prism, similarly students of CET consist of different skills within them and Spectrum acts like a prism, Spectrum recognizes their skills and helps to strengthen them.

This club was established by " Bikram Keshari Panda (2015 batch) ".Spectrum got its recognition in the year 2016 and got its workspace, funding and other facilities. Primarily this was his idea to have an electronics society in our college but with the efforts of other members we established two other wings - Software and Design.The other seniors who had their valuable contribution for this initiative were: Ankit Mishra, Zighnesh Biswal, Abhishek Dash, Avinash Nayak, Tapan Kumar Pal, Krutikesh Sahoo, Abhilash Das.

Our Achievements

What we have achieved together

Texas Instruments India Innovation Challenge

Two groups of the club has qualified for the Texas Instruments India Innovation Challenge.

India International Science Festival

Smart Toilet for physically challenged has been awarded fourth in a National Level Competition in India International Science Festival conducted by CSIR, NIOT and IIT Madras.

Smart India Hackathon 2018

One group of the club has qualified for the Smart India Hackathon 2018.

Hackathon by Sankalp Semiconductors

Three students of our club had been awarded 3rd in a hackathon conducted by Sankalp Semiconductors.

11 publications in IEEE Xplore and Springer

There are total 11 number of publications in IEEE Xplore and Springer by the club members in the field of Control and Automation, Industrial Electronics and Soft Computing.

DRUSE 2018 conducted by DRDO

Four of our members were selected for the first round in DRUSE 2018 conducted by DRDO.

Hack with Infy conducted by Infosys

One member of the club has won the Hack with Infy conducted by Infosys.

Robotics Championship conducted by Infosys

One member of the club has been the second runners up in Robotics Championship conducted by Infosys.

Tata Steel Woman of Mettle

One of the female member of our club has been awarded as Tata Steel Woman of Mettle and has been awarded an internship along with PPO.

Nautilus Championship conducted by IIT, Guwahati

One of the group of our club has won the Nautilus Championship conducted by IIT, Guwahati.

Facebook Hackercup

A student of our club qualified for the Facebook Hackercup.
